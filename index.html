About

Hack4Humanity is an IEEE HAC funded competition organized by IA/IE/PELS Jt. Chapter Kerala where individual participants are required to find a solution to the given problem statement within a limited period of time. The problem statements consist of questions based on the SDGs. Each problem statement will be provided on individual days and the best solution will be selected for the next phase. The selected participants will be formed into groups of four members. The following event is again followed by two phases.

The second phase is an Ideathon where the teams are supposed to discuss and formulate an idea based on the given problem. The best ideas will be selected and the teams are taken to the Third phase. The third phase is a Hackathon where the students are made to bring up a prototype of their ideas.
